My Goal

The firewall is designed to be used in conjunction with a Raspberry Pi. It could also be used with another computer device, or a wireless router. The purpose of this firewall is not to provide 100% protection against hacker attacks but instead to provide an additional layer of security so that unauthorized use by other devices cannot be accomplished. The idea behind configuring the firewall is to restrict access as much as possible in your local network to unauthorized devices. This will also prevent unauthorized access via remote devices, such as internet hackers and surveillance.

Requirements

In order to use the Raspberry Pi in this project, you will need a few additional components.

  • Raspberry Pi with a Model A or Model B
  • A computer monitor (HDMI) and the appropriate cable (HDMI)
  • A power source that is capable of delivering 5 volts and 2 amps. You can use the AC Adapter that came with your Raspberry Pi
  • A microSD card
  • Ethernet cables
  • A Wi-Fi network to connect the Raspberry Pi to
  • A computer/laptop for configuring the Raspberry Pi
